coping with natural disasters

About
We created this game to make players aware of the nature and environment we are losing. Our game simulates the conservation of this resource. If we conserve it, what will we find? If we don't conserve it, how will we end up with it?
objective
- Make players aware of the resources that are about to be wasted
- Simulate a brighter world if we preserve this environment
how to play
1. Open the game
2. Press start
3. For new players, press New.
4. Read the story that the game has prepared.
5. Choose the solution that matches the problem.
6. Choose to solve the problem for 30 days in the game to win. During the 30 days there will be an achievement such as preventing the eruption of a volcano or saving all people from the danger.
7. Choose to continue playing or this is enough.
8. Share playing statistics with friends.
